About Us

We love coffee and we love community. That is why we thought it would be a great to combine the two together. All of our menu items are of the best quality made by skilled volunteers. We offer all our items through "Suggested Donation". You can pay more, you can pay less, or you can have your menu item on us. We just love making folks smile. All proceeds from our menu items allow us to keep Kairos Koffeehouse in operation with the majority of those proceeds going to a local charity, This semester, we will be partnering with the Southeastern Louisiana University Food Pantry. As you enjoy your menu item, know that someone else will be benefiting from your investment.
